Recommend

29th of Feb was awful. I went to a local bar and turned down a couple of beer's, and headed to meet a client. Shortly after I concluded the business, I was heading home and parked in a neighborhood bar. I gulped 3 large pegs of rum. As I was prepared to leave, I discovered that my credit card did not operate. I did not have money. Nor I had an appropriate balance on my debit card. Thus, I summoned a buddy to meet my bill. Right at this moment, I was agitated, disgusted, and felt emotionally unhappy. Fuck this habit of mine -- drinking! I told myself "Come on take control of your life, and quit your age-old habits!"

Tracking my progress for next 22 days.

March 1st, 2016
This is where it all began. Deep within, I feel beautiful as I've decided to quit drinking. I still smell the aroma of success, and I feel fascinating. The day went as I'd planned. I did not drink. One habit changed. Heck, I even switched to a plain and simple diet - boil the veggies, top them with salt and pepper and relish! Success!

March 2nd, 2016
I sense excitement and joyfulness. Boy oh boy, I'm 16 again!
Today, I started adding bits to my calendar to keep track of my hourly progress.
Tremendous stuff. I'm trying to be organized. I love the control!
The day went as I'd planned. Shortly after my breakfast, I rushed to coach a batch of young, and talented web developers. I went home by late evening and watched a few videos. Later in the evening, boiled a few stands of spinach, a potato, and three chilies topped them with a bit of salt, pepper and ate a simple, and healthy meal.
